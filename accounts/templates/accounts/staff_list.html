Understanding roles

Each role comes with a default set of permissions. You can adjust individual permissions by clicking Edit on any staff member.

Doctor / Admin
Full access to all features — reception, prescriptions, pharmacy, analytics, and staff management.
Receptionist
Patient registration and queue management only. No access to prescriptions, pharmacy, or reports.
Pharmacist
Pharmacy, stock management, and patient billing only. No access to prescriptions or clinical reports.
